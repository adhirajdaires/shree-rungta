❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I place an order? ▼
Browse our products, click "Add to Cart", then click the Cart button and fill in your details. We'll confirm your order on WhatsApp and arrange delivery!
How will I get my order? ▼
After you place an order online, our team will contact you on WhatsApp to confirm product availability, final price, and arrange delivery or pickup at our store in Chakuliya.
Do you accept online payments? ▼
Payment is collected at delivery or pickup. We accept cash, UPI, and other modes. Our team will guide you on WhatsApp after order confirmation.
What is your return / exchange policy? ▼
We offer exchange or return within 3 days of delivery for damaged or incorrect items. Please contact us via WhatsApp or phone with a photo of the product issue.
Can I visit the store directly? ▼
Absolutely! Visit us at Purana Bazar, Main Road, Near Vivek Medical, Rungta House, Chakuliya, Jharkhand. We'd love to see you in person!
Are the food products homemade? ▼
Yes! Our pickles, haldi powder, and papad are all lovingly homemade with authentic recipes — no preservatives, pure taste.
How do I track my order? ▼
Once your order is confirmed, we'll keep you updated via WhatsApp on every step — from confirmation to delivery.

✦ Our Story ✦
"Apka Apna Mart — where every product feels like it was picked just for you."

Shree Rungta is a beloved neighbourhood mart nestled in the heart of Chakuliya, Jharkhand. We started with a simple vision — to bring quality, variety, and warmth together under one roof for our community.

From elegant home décor and premium fragrances to handmade pickles and children's toys — every item we stock is chosen with care and purpose. We believe that a good product should not just be useful, but should bring a smile.
